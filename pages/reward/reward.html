Frequently asked questions

  • What is the BOJ Rewards Program?+
    The BOJ Rewards Program is our way of showing appreciation to our valued customers. By participating, you can earn Gems with every purchase, which can be redeemed for special discounts, exclusive products, free shipping, and more. As you collect Gems and advance through tiers, you unlock even greater rewards!
  • Can I get Gems from my past orders before signing up?+
    Welcome to BOJ Rewards! We're thrilled to have you with us. Please note that Gems are only credited from the moment you officially join our rewards program. Once enrolled, you’ll start earning Gems on all future purchases.
  • How do I join the rewards program?+
    Joining is easy! Simply visit our rewards page on the global mall, locate the 'BOJ Rewards' section, and complete the sign-up process. As a bonus, you'll receive 150 additional Gems for referring a friend. Invite your friends to enjoy BOJ's exclusive benefits together!
  • What benefits come with tier upgrades?+
    Extra Birthday Gems: Celebrate your special day with extra Gems, which increase as you move up the tiers. Exclusive Merchandise and More: Pearl and Sapphire tier members gain access to exclusive merchandise. Pearl & Sapphire members also receive an annual special gift.
  • Does my tier expire?+
    At the end of 12 months after achieving your current VIP tier, we will review whether the required spending amount was met over the past year to maintain your status. If not, you may be moved to a lower VIP tier.
